Research Interest

 I work in the field of remote sensing image quality improvement, low-level vision, multi-source remote sensing information fusion and deep learning theory. Currently, I focus on the following research topics:
  • Combined data-driven with model-driven strategy for low-level vision
  • Thick cloud removal in multi-temporal remote sensing images
  • Time-series remote sensing product reconstruction
  • Hyperspectral image denoising and restoration
  • Tensor factorization and deep learning
